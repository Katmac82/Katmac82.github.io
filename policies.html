Policies

For the benefit and respect of Love Pole Fitness and our students please review the terms and policies regarding cancellation of classes and membership.

24 hour notice is required if you are not able to attend a class you have signed up for. Failure to text, call or email will result in the following:

  • Unlimited month students will be charged $25 per class missed
  • Punch card holders will lose a class on their card per class missed

When signing up for an unlimited membership please be aware that you are receiving an extensive discount for committing to 6 or 12 months. You are under contract for the duration of the time outlined when signing up and are obligated to pay on time each month. Declined card transactions will result in a $25 fee and any cancellation of membership will have a $75 fee. These guildlines are to benefit and protect both the teacher and the students to keep Love Pole Fitness a fun, functional and fabulous place to work out! Thank you for understanding and we look forward to always sharing our LOVE.

There is a minimum requirement of 3 students per class. Any classes that have less than this amount are subject to be canceled at any time. Students are able to sign up for classes up to 1 week in advance, and 1 hour before scheduled class. Any cancellations done by LOVE will not result in fees and class will be able to be made up.

Please no phone calls, texting, videos, or photos in class without the permission of the instructor.

If a student is more than 10 minutes late for class they will not be able to participate. The classes offered at LOVE can be rigorous and missing warm up is not safe.

Signing up for class is to be done at www.mindbodyonline.com. Teachers are not responsible for signing up, or canceling students for any classes. Issues with Mindbody can be emailed to lovepolefitness@hotmail.com
