Our Philosophy

At LOVE Pole Fitness our goal is to offer a safe, comfortable, and above all, fun exercise environment. We invite you let down your hair and get a total body work out from one of our many exercise classes. We are now offering Pole fitness, Aerial Hoops, Aerial Silks, Chair Dance, Circuit Training, Belly Dance, and more. Each class offers a cardio blast combined with all over muscle toning. With every class you will improve your confidence, self-esteem, and body awareness. Our studio is primarily for Women only, of any size, age, or fitness level, however we are now offering some CO-ED classes. Whether you are looking to lose weight, tone up, or even just make new friends, LOVE's flirty, fun, and fitness sanctuary is just for you!
